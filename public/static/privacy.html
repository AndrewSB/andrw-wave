Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 7, 2026

This is a placeholder privacy policy for Dock Me. It is provided for product testing and publication scaffolding, and should be reviewed before being treated as legal, compliance, or App Store submission language.

Overview

Dock Me is a lightweight watch app that helps riders find nearby Citi Bike docking availability. The app is designed to use only the information needed to show nearby dock options and improve the experience.

Information We Collect

Dock Me may process the following information:

  • Approximate location coordinates when you ask the app to find nearby stations.
  • Basic request details, such as the time of a request and technical diagnostic information generated by the hosting provider.
  • Feedback you choose to submit, including audio feedback if that feature is available and you decide to use it.

How We Use Information

We use information to:

  • Find and display nearby stations with available docks.
  • Operate, secure, debug, and improve the service.
  • Review optional feedback submitted by users.

Location Data

Location data is used to return nearby station results. Dock Me does not intentionally sell location data, build advertising profiles, or track your movement history. The app may send coordinates to the Dock Me worker service so it can calculate nearby station options.

Third-Party Services

Dock Me may rely on third-party infrastructure providers to host the worker service, process network requests, or deliver optional feedback. The app may also request public bike-share station information from services associated with Citi Bike or its operators. Those third parties may process data under their own policies.

Data Retention

We keep personal information only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, unless a longer period is required for security, debugging, legal, or operational reasons. Optional feedback may be retained so we can review and act on it.

Sharing

We do not intentionally sell personal information. We may share information with service providers that help operate Dock Me, when required by law, or when needed to protect the rights, safety, and security of users, the service, or others.

Children

Dock Me is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Your Choices

You can control location access through your device settings. If you do not grant location access, nearby dock features may not work as intended. You can also choose whether to submit optional feedback.

Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. If we make changes, we will update the date at the top of this page.
